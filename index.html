THE TRIPLE P CODE

01. PRAY

Spiritual Connection. The foundation of our success is our connection to the Creator. We prioritize Salah, mindfulness, and inner discipline before outward action.

02. PLAY

Physical Strength. "The strong believer is better than the weak." We engage in Halal fitness, Sunnah sports, and recreation to build bodies capable of greatness.

03. PROGRESS

Continuous Growth. We strive for excellence (Ihsan) in our careers, education, and character. We level up in the Dunya to serve the Ummah.
