About Us

Our Story

Triple P Squad began as a platform for positive youth development in Pakistan under Al-Burhan. We recognized the need for a holistic approach to nurturing young Muslims who are grounded in faith, physically active, and equipped with practical life skills.

Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals who can navigate the challenges of modern life while staying true to Islamic values and principles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the time commitment? +

The program requires attendance at Sunday sessions (4-6 hours) and occasional weekend activities. We understand academic commitments and offer flexibility when needed.

Is there a registration fee? +

Yes, there is a one-time registration fee of PKR 2,000 which covers administrative costs and program materials. Financial assistance is available for those in need.

Where are the sessions held? +

We have centers in Karachi, Islamabad, and Lahore. Specific locations vary based on activities - indoor sessions at our centers, outdoor activities at designated parks and facilities.

What safety measures are in place? +

All activities are supervised by trained mentors. We maintain appropriate staff-to-youth ratios, conduct background checks on all volunteers, and follow strict safety protocols for all physical activities.
